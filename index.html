Wordsmith

Welcome to my Wordsmith game!

New here? The game is pretty simple!

  1. Use the "Random Vowel" button to add a random vowel to the board
  2. Use the "Random Consonant" button to add a random consonant to the board
  3. Keep pressing either button until you have a variety of vowels and consonants on the board
  4. When all 10 spaces are populated, the timer will begin
  5. You now have 30 seconds to list as many words using the letters shown as you can!
  6. The longer the word, the more points you get!
  7. Words 5 characters or longer get a bonus 5 points
  8. Different characters have different values! (just like "Scrabble")
  9. 1 point for A, E, I, L, N, O, R, S, T, and U
  10. 2 points for D and G.
  11. 3 points for B, C, M, and P
  12. 4 points for F, H, V, W, and Y
  13. 5 points for K
  14. 8 points for J and X
  15. 10 points for Q and Z
  16. When you are ready, navigate to the menu on the left and click "Start"
  17. Have fun!
